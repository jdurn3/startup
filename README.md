# ClimbCoord

## Elevator Pitch

Have dozens of climbing trips that have never made it out of the group chat? ClimbCoord is your ultimate companion for seamless climbing adventures! ClimbCoord is a group calendering platform designed for passionate climbers to effortlessly plan their next ascent and join other's trips. Connect with fellow climbers, coordinate gear, and assess skill levels with ease. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned pro, our platform empowers you to meet new climbers and make your climbing goals a reality! ## Key Features
- Secure login over HTTPS
- Group Calender listing trips of connected friends. 
- A place to sign up for other's trips. 
- A general itinerary/description of each trip. 
- Listed details such as skill level, gear needed, and climb difficulty. 
- Ability to chat with climbing partners.
